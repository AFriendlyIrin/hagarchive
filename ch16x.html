Chapter 16x: Perfect World

This chapter is accessed by accepting the deal in Chapter 16, which can only be done on a repeat playthrough.

The base camp is not accessible this chapter. The armory is, but you will deal with a generic blacksmith instead of Hestia.

The enemy units in this chapter have the unique sprites and personal skills of the main cast, but none of them have portraits or dialogue and are treated as generic units (with a few exceptions listed in the character descriptions).

The clear condition for this chapter reads “Kill them all.”
